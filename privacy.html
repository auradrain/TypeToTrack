Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

TypeToTrack ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the TypeToTrack mobile application.

Information You Provide

When using TypeToTrack, you may provide:

  • Meal descriptions entered as text or voice
  • Optional weight data (via Apple Health, if enabled)
  • Basic profile information such as age, height, and gender (used only to calculate nutrition goals)

How Your Information Is Used

Your meal descriptions are processed by our backend AI system to generate nutritional estimates (calories, macros, and micronutrients).

Profile information (age, height, gender) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

If you enable Apple Health integration, weight data is read from Apple Health only to display progress within the app. We do not store or transmit Health data outside your device.

Data Storage

Meal logs and profile data are stored locally on your device. Meal text submitted for AI analysis is transmitted securely to our servers for processing and returned with nutritional information.

Third-Party Services

TypeToTrack uses third-party AI services to process meal descriptions for nutritional analysis. These services process data solely to generate nutrition results and do not use your information for advertising.

Subscriptions are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. We do not have access to your payment details.

Data Security

We use secure HTTPS connections to transmit data. While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You may disable Apple Health access at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You may delete the app to remove all locally stored data.

Children’s Privacy

TypeToTrack is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
